Risk Moments

When Autonomous Safety Fails

Standard monitoring assumes behavior is constant. In reality, autonomous agents are safe until they aren't. We focus on the moments where visibility disappears.

  • Silent Model Drift: When yesterday's safe output becomes today's compliance incident through gradual, undetected semantic shifts.
  • Runaway Automation: When an agent behaves correctly... until a multi-step loop executes actions in a black box without an audit trail.
  • Policy Violations: When real-time breaches occurs because human review cannot scale to the speed of agent interaction.
  • Intent Opacity: Knowing what an agent did, but having zero visibility into the logic that bypassed your guardrails.

What Is AgentProtector?

A Persistent, Intelligent Observer

AgentProtector is not a controller, nor a chatbot. It is a dedicated, independent AI entity that sits adjacent to your operational AI systems. It possesses the context of your policies, the logic of your compliance framework, and the intelligence to audit autonomous decisions as they happen.

Why Now?

Human-in-the-Loop Does Not Scale

As enterprises deploy hundreds of specialized agents, manual human review becomes the bottleneck that creates the most risk.

Compliance Gaps

Regulations like the EU AI Act require continuous monitoring, not just point-in-time audits.

Agent Autonomy

When agents interact with other agents, the speed of decision-making outpaces human intervention.

Real-World Impact

In financial or legal sectors, a single undetected drift can lead to systemic failure or massive liability.
